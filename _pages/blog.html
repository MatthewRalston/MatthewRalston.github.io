Bridging Software and Biology: Technology and Genomics in the age of AI

The focus of this blog is topics on machine-learning, AI, Genomics, Phylogenomics, Bioinformatic methods, data analysis and statistical methods for interpreting molecular and/or genomic assays. I review topics on AI, statistics, probability, linear algebra, multiplexed expression profiling methods, regression, 'meta-programming' and old-school principles first coding, TDD, DSA, applications programming and new-school AI-assisted software engineering. You may find poetry, prose, and other tech news related discussions. I work on a project related to minimizers, k-mers, alignment and quasi-mapping and that is often visited in this blog as well.
